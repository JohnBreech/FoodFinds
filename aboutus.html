About Us

FoodFinds connects students and the campus community to nearby food stalls, vendors, and pop-ups—making great food easy to discover. Our mission is to reduce food waste, promote local businesses, and help you find delicious options in just a few taps.

We curate menus, locations, and hours in one place so you can focus on what matters: eating well without the hassle. Whether you’re on a budget or in a hurry, FoodFinds puts access to food at your fingertips.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FoodFinds? ▾
FoodFinds is a centralized digital directory for the Ateneo de Zamboanga University cafeteria. We help students and faculty browse menus, compare prices, and check operating hours remotely, saving you the hassle of walking between floors to decide what to eat.
Why are some cafeteria stalls missing? ▾
We are currently in the prototype phase (School Year 2025-2026), featuring 5 select food stalls. We plan to expand our directory to include all authorized cafeteria vendors in future updates as the project develops.
Can I order food directly through the website? ▾
No. FoodFinds is currently an informational platform only. Our goal is to help you decide what to eat before you arrive at the cafeteria. You will still need to visit the stall physically to purchase your meal.
Who is FoodFinds designed for? ▾
FoodFinds is built for the entire Ateneo de Zamboanga University community. Whether you are a student, faculty member, or staff, our goal is to make your dining experience easier by putting the cafeteria directory in your pocket.
